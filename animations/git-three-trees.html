🌳 Git's Three Trees — Interactive Simulator

The #1 thing to understand about Git. Click the buttons and watch your file travel through the three areas. No installation, no risk — just learn by doing.

📦 Real-world analogy: Think of shipping a parcel. Working Directory = your desk where you pack things (messy, changing). Staging Area = the box by the door you've decided to actually send. Repository = the parcel handed to the post office — sealed, dated, and permanently recorded. git add moves things into the box; git commit seals & ships it.
