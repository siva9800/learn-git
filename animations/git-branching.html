🌿 Branching, Merge & Rebase — See the Difference

Branches let many people work at once without stepping on each other. The hardest question — "merge or rebase?" — becomes obvious once you see it. Build a history below, then try both.

📖 Analogy: A branch is like writing a chapter draft in a separate notebook so you don't scribble over the published book. Merge = staple both notebooks together with a note saying "combined here" (keeps the real timeline, adds a merge commit). Rebase = neatly recopy your draft pages onto the end of the published book so it reads as one clean straight story (rewrites history).
